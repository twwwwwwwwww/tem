GET TO KNOW

THE NETHERLANDS

52.1326°N,
5.2913°E

The Netherlands is a country in Northeastern Europe, next to Belgium and Germany. The capital city of the Netherlands is Amsterdam, popular for its architecture, art, and canals. The official language is Dutch, but 90% of the population can speak English, which highly appeals to internationals!

Dutch people are known to have the tallest average height in the world, and scientists actually believe this is because the country is rich with milk and cheese. The Netherlands is a beautiful country with great people despite having gloomy weather most of the time. It has an interesting history and ongoing innovations.

TRAVEL

DESTINATIONS

Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital city of the Netherlands. It is a vibrant and diverse city known for its nightlife, exciting art scene, and beautiful architecture.

Rotterdam

Rotterdam is one of the most unique cities in the Netherlands. Unlike anywhere else, the architecture in Rotterdam is more modern and unique than historical.

Delft

Delft is a small city rich with Dutch culture. They are most famous for their hand-painted blue-and-white Delftware and ringed-canals found all throughout the city.

The Hague

The Hague is a historical city popular in the summertime because of their beach. The city is also home to the U.N.’s International Court of Justice.

Groningen

Groningen is in the North of the Netherlands and is known for their beautiful and historic monuments and countryside aesthetic as well as vibrant student life.

Zaandvort

Zandvoort is known for their beautiful beaches and sand dunes, found nowhere else in the country. The name Zandvoort translates to Seaside Pearl.

ARTS

&

CULTURE

Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh is a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter who became one of the most influential figures in Western art history. He created over 2,000 artworks which continue to receive international interests and spark various conversations. Although he was not well recognized during his time, he now is a key figure of the Netherlands.

You can learn more about his life and see his iconic pieces at the Van Gogh Museum!

Visit the Van Gogh Museum

Rembrandt van Rijn

Rembrandt van Rijn is a beloved painter from the Dutch Golden Age. He was a master in three different types of media which covered a vast array of subject matters and still is considered one of the best painters in European art history. It is believed that he has created over 300 paintings, 300 etchings and 2,000 sketches.

You can learn more about his life and find his work at the Rembrandt Museumhuis!

Visit Rembrandt Museumhuis

Anne Frank

Anne Frank is a German-born Jewish girl who wrote a diary about her life under Nazi persecution. The very house with the secret annex that she hid in is located in Amsterdam, and the house has been preserved in memory of the figure and history. Anne Frank House is a highly recommended historical attraction to visit when in Amsterdam.

You can learn more about Anne Frank’s life at the Anne Frank Huis!

Visit Anne Frank House

MODE

OF

TRANSPORT

#biking #bicycle #cycle

As a country that cares about and takes action for sustainability and greener lifestyles, the main mode of transportation in the Netherlands reflects how the general public participate in a daily activity that truly causes no harm to the environment.

Biking is the main mode of transportation in the Netherlands (perhaps another reason why people are so tall). It is the most environmentally friendly, fast, and healthy way to get around. There are several bicycle rental places all over the country that are easily accessible.

LET'S
BIKE!

It is more time and energy efficient for people to travel by bike. There is a special traffic-calming system for bikers and safe roads that also greatly reduce any accidents that may occur. Surprisingly few bike-related accidents occur in the Netherlands as a result.

Bike around Vondelpark and enjoy the nature.

#biking #vondelpark #nature #park

Vondelpark is one of the most famous parks in the Netherlands, located in the heart of Amsterdam. When the sun is out and shining, one of the best ways to enjoy the rare occasion is to be outside! We recommend you visiting Vondelpark and biking around the area to take in the nature.

Experience the art scene in the Netherlands.

#art #artscene #museumplein #museums

If you are an art lover, it is a must to experience the vast art scene in the Netherlands. A good place to start off is to visit Museumplein, also in the heart of Amsterdam, where all the iconic museums surround you. you will be able to experience art throughout history in one setting.

Visit the tulip fields.

#tulip #tulipfields #spring #keukenhof

Tulips are symbolic of the Netherlands. When springtime approaches, all the tulip fields throughout the Netherlands bloom and look beautiful. Keukenhof is a well-known tulip field as well as park that you can walk around and enjoy the sight of the flowers, but there are other fields as well.
